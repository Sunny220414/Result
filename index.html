OUR LEGACY

FIREBOLT RACING was founded in 2009 and ever since the team has achieved great success, consistently placing at the top of the competitor pool. Every year we compete in BAJA SAE INDIA, BAJA SAE INTERNATIONAL, MEGA ATV and other major events. We take pride in accomplishments like being one of two teams to implement Continuously Variable Transmission in BAJA SAE INDIA 2012, earning recognition from Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam. None of this would be possible without the tireless efforts of every team member across technical, electrical, and managerial domains.
