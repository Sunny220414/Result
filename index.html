OUR LEGACY

FIREBOLT RACING was founded in 2009 and ever since the team has achieved great success, consistently placing at the top of the competitor pool in the past years. Every year we compete In BAJA SAE INDIA, BAJA SAE INTERNATIONAL, MEGA ATV and other major events where we are tested in various categories. We take pride in our accomplishments like becoming one of the two teams who successfully implemented Continuously Variable Transmission in their ATV in BAJA SAE INDIA 2012 and being appreciated by Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM and getting special mention by judges in 2012. And none of this could have been achieved without the contribution each and every member of the team who have put in countless hours of dedicated work on the technical, electrical and managerial sector of the vehicles and we look forward to continuing our hard work in the coming years.
